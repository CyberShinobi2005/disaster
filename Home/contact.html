Support & FAQ

Find answers to common questions.

How can I request help? expand_more

You can request help by filling out the form on our 'Request Help' page or by calling our emergency helpline. Please provide as much detail as possible so we can assist you effectively.

How can I donate? expand_more

We appreciate your support! You can donate through our secure online portal on the 'Donate' page. We accept various payment methods. Your contribution goes directly to funding our relief efforts.

How can I volunteer? expand_more

Volunteers are the backbone of our organization. Please visit the 'Volunteer' page to see current opportunities and fill out the application form. We'll get in touch with you for orientation and training.
