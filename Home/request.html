Frequently Asked Questions

How fast will I get help?

Our teams are working around the clock. We prioritize the most critical cases and aim to respond as quickly as possible. Response times can vary depending on the scale of the disaster and accessibility of the area.

Who will contact me?

A verified ReliefHub volunteer or a team member from one of our partner organizations will contact you on the phone number you provided. Please keep your phone lines open.

Is my information safe?

Yes, your privacy and security are important to us. The information you provide will only be used by our team to coordinate relief efforts and will not be shared with third parties.
